Being A First Year Student At OsloMet

Together we have built this webpage to give you a better understanding on what it is like to be a first year student at OsloMet.

As first a first year student it is really important to make connections, and find the students that are matching you ambition level. If not you will find yourself frustrated over the fact that they do not put in the same amount of effort as you do.

You will have to learn how to have some fun with all the cool stuff that you learn. You can see us playing around on the fun section of the website!
